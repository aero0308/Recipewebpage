Masala Dosa

    Ingredients:-

  • Rice (for dosa batter)
  • Urad dal (split black lentils)(for dosa batter)
  • Boiled potatoes (for the filling)
  • Onions (for the filling)
  • Mustard seeds (for tempering)
  • Turmeric powder (for flavoring the filling)

    Recipe:-

  • Soak and grind rice, urad dal, and fenugreek for batter; ferment overnight.
  • Make potato filling by sautéing onions, mustard seeds, and spices.
  • Cook dosa on tawa, add potato filling, and fold.

Masala Pasta (Indian Style)

    Ingredients:-

  • Pasta (penne, fusilli, or any preferred type)
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Tomatoes (chopped)
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Red chili powder or garam masala
  • Vegetables (like capsicum, peas, or carrots)

    Recipe:-

  • Boil pasta.
  • Sauté onions, tomatoes, and ginger-garlic paste.
  • Add veggies and spices, cook until soft.
  • Mix boiled pasta and toss.

Manchurian

    Ingredients:-

  • Cabbage (grated or finely chopped)
  • Carrot (grated)
  • All-purpose flour (Maida)
  • Cornflour
  • Soy sauce
  • Ginger-Garlic paste

    Recipe:-

  • Mix cabbage, cornflour, salt, and spices. Shape into balls and deep-fry.
  • Sauté ginger-garlic, add soy sauce, chili sauce, ketchup, and water.
  • Toss fried balls in the sauce and cook for a minute. Serve hot.
